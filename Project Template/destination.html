Top 10

Ibiza

Ibiza is a city located on the Balearic Islands, it is known for its amazing sights, attractions and night life.

Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates and is very famous for its sightseeing attractions.

Galway

Galway is a city in County Galway in the West of Ireland that has very popular sightseeing destinations.

The city of Miami has trendy nightlife, beaches, art galleries and world-class hotels.

Turkey is known for having a huge variety and type of places! From amazing beaches through its huge coastline to big mountain ranges.

France

France is well known for its gourmet food, famous French artists and is also a very popular tourist hotspot.
